Appointment Scheduling

Scheduling Requirements:

All appointments must follow our scheduling guidelines to ensure availability and proper preparation.

  • Site visits must be scheduled at least 24 hours in advance
  • Appointments are available Monday through Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
  • Sunday visits are available by special arrangement only
  • Each visit is limited to a maximum of 2 hours
  • Late arrivals (more than 15 minutes) may result in appointment cancellation
Pro Tip:

Book your preferred time slot early, especially during peak seasons. Popular viewing times fill up quickly!

Visitor Requirements

Mandatory Requirements:

All visitors must meet these requirements. No exceptions will be made for security and safety reasons.

  • Valid government-issued photo identification required for all visitors
  • Maximum of 4 people per site visit group
  • All visitors must be 18 years of age or older
  • Visitors under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian
  • Business attire or casual dress code required (no shorts, flip-flops, or revealing clothing)

Safety & Liability

Safety First:

Your safety is our priority. Please follow all safety guidelines and instructions from our staff.

  • Visitors participate at their own risk and liability
  • Closed-toe shoes are mandatory for all site visits
  • Hard hats and safety equipment will be provided when necessary
  • Visitors must follow all safety instructions from the tour guide
  • The property owner/agent is not liable for accidents or injuries during the visit
  • Visitors with medical conditions should inform the guide before the tour begins

Photography & Recording

Photography Guidelines:

Capture memories while respecting privacy and property rules.

  • Photography for personal use is permitted unless otherwise specified
  • Commercial photography requires prior written consent
  • Video recording may be restricted in certain areas
  • Respect privacy of current tenants if property is occupied
  • Social media posting should be done responsibly and respectfully

Professional Conduct

Respectful Behavior:

Maintain professionalism to ensure a positive experience for everyone involved.

  • Maintain respectful and professional behavior throughout the visit
  • Do not touch or move personal belongings of current tenants
  • Ask permission before opening cabinets, closets, or appliances
  • Keep noise levels appropriate, especially in occupied buildings
  • Smoking, vaping, and alcohol consumption are strictly prohibited
  • Pets are not allowed during site visits unless it's a service animal
